# Social & Diplomacy

This document summarizes the social structures of OGate from the HLDD: **Faction Alignment** (the main social glue in the early game) and the **Stellar Concordat** (daily PvE and home security). There are **no player Corporations in the early game**; Corporations and player-owned outposts belong to Phase 2 / Void Capacitor content (HLDD §3.2, §4.1). The same systems are summarized in **Home System** (docs/02_home_system.md); they support the **Coffee Break** and **Progression** steps of the Macro Loop (docs/01_core_loops.md).

---

## 1. Faction Alignment (The Social Glue)

The galaxy is populated by **major AI factions**. Players **pledge loyalty** to a specific Faction. This drives social cohesion without the administrative overhead of a player-run Corp (HLDD §3.2).

### Faction Chat

- Pledging grants access to a shared **Faction Chat**, allowing **coordination and social play** (HLDD §3.2).

### Collaborative Research

- Faction members **contribute resources** to a global **“Faction Research Pool”** (HLDD §3.2).
- When **milestones are hit**, **all members** gain (HLDD §3.2):
  - **Temporary buffs** (e.g. “+10% Mining Yield for 24h”), or
  - **Exclusive Faction Blueprints**.

### Faction War Events (Weekly)

- **Weekly global events** where **two Factions go to war** (HLDD §3.2).
- Players contribute by completing **specific PvE objectives**. HLDD examples: “Donate 10,000 Iron,” “Destroy 50 Pirate Ships.”
- Progress is **aggregate and global**. **Rewards are distributed weekly** based on the Faction’s success (HLDD §3.2).

*Note:* The HLDD does not name individual factions, specify pledge cooldowns/penalties, or describe in-instance “friendly fire” indicators. This doc only summarizes what is in the HLDD.

## 2. The Stellar Concordat (Home Security)

The **Stellar Concordat** is an **interstellar alliance dedicated to the safety of developed systems** (akin to Concord) (HLDD §3.2).

### Security Contracts (Daily PvE)

- The Concordat issues **daily PvE quests** to the player (HLDD §3.2).
- **HLDD examples:** “Eliminate Pirate Scouts,” “Rescue Diplomatic Shuttle,” “Scan Anomalies” (HLDD §3.2).

### Rewards: Concordat Commendations

- Completing contracts grants **Concordat Commendations**, a specialized currency (HLDD §3.2).
- Commendations are used to purchase (HLDD §3.2):
  - **Defensive orbital platforms** (for the Home System),
  - **Safe-logistics ships**,
  - **Temporary AI wingmen** (hire for sorties).

This provides a **safe, constructive daily loop** when the player does not want to risk a PvP sortie, and ties into the **Coffee Break** and **Progression** steps in the Macro Loop (docs/01_core_loops.md).
